How to Set Up Call Forwarding on Airtel (2026) — USSD Codes + Airtel Thanks App

All Airtel USSD codes, the Airtel Thanks app method, troubleshooting for common issues, and how to connect your Katch AI number in 60 seconds.

Quick Answer

To set up call forwarding on Airtel: dial **67*[number]# (forward when busy) and **61*[number]# (forward when no answer). Press call after each. Or use the Airtel Thanks app: Menu → Account Settings → Calls → Call Forwarding. To cancel all: ##002#. Replace [number] with your Katch AI number from the app.

Method 2: Airtel Thanks App

If USSD codes don't work on your plan, use the Airtel Thanks app:

  1. 1. Open the Airtel Thanks app
  2. 2. Tap the Menu ☰ icon (top left or bottom nav)
  3. 3. Tap Account Settings (or My Account)
  4. 4. Tap Calls
  5. 5. Tap Call Forwarding
  6. 6. Enter your Katch number for When Busy and When No Answer
  7. 7. Save and verify the setting is active

Troubleshooting Airtel Call Forwarding

USSD says "Service Unavailable"

Use the Airtel Thanks app method instead. Some prepaid plans restrict USSD call forwarding activation via dialler.

Airtel 5G users

On Airtel 5G, USSD forwarding may require switching to 4G first. Go to Settings → Mobile Network → Preferred network type → 4G, set up forwarding, then switch back.

No confirmation received

Try restarting your phone after dialling. Verify status by dialling *#67#.

Forwarding not working on roaming

Contact Airtel care at 121 (free from Airtel) to verify international call forwarding is enabled on your plan before travelling.

How to Cancel Airtel Call Forwarding
