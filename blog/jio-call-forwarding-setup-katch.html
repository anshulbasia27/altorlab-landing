How to Set Up Call Forwarding on Jio (2026) — Step by Step

All Jio USSD codes, the MyJio app method, troubleshooting tips, and how to connect your Katch AI number in 60 seconds.

Quick Answer

To set up call forwarding on Jio: dial **67*[number]# (forward when busy) and **61*[number]# (forward when no answer). Press call after each. Wait for confirmation SMS. To cancel all: ##002#. Replace [number] with your Katch AI number from the app.

MyJio App Alternative

If USSD codes don't work on your plan, use the MyJio app:

  1. 1. Open the MyJio app
  2. 2. Tap More (bottom nav)
  3. 3. Tap Account Settings
  4. 4. Tap Call Settings
  5. 5. Tap Call Forwarding
  6. 6. Enter your Katch number for "When Busy" and "When No Answer"

Troubleshooting Common Issues

USSD code says "Not Allowed"

Some Jio prepaid plans restrict USSD call forwarding. Use the MyJio app method instead, or contact Jio care on 199.

No confirmation SMS received

Try restarting your phone after dialling the code. Check if you heard a voice confirmation during the call — that also counts.

Forwarding to international number

Include the full country code — e.g. **67*+91XXXXXXXXXX# for Indian numbers. Katch numbers use Indian country codes.

Calls not forwarding in tests

Verify with *#67# to check status. Make sure you set both **67* (busy) and **61* (no answer) — you need both for full coverage.
