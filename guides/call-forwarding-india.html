All USSD codes, step-by-step setup for every major Indian carrier, plus how to connect Katch AI voicemail in 60 seconds.

Quick Answer

To set up call forwarding in India: Jio — dial **67*[number]# when busy or **61*[number]# when no answer. Airtel — same codes or use the Airtel Thanks app. Vodafone/Vi — same codes. Cancel all: ##002#. Replace [number] with your Katch AI number from the app.

How to Check and Cancel Call Forwarding

Check if active: Dial *#67# on most carriers to see if conditional (when busy) forwarding is active.

Cancel all forwarding: Dial ##002# to remove all forwarding on Jio, Airtel, or Vodafone Vi.

Your regular calls are completely unaffected — forwarding only activates when you're busy or don't answer.

Does Call Forwarding Cost Money in India?

Conditional call forwarding (when busy or no answer) is free on Jio, Airtel, Vodafone Vi, and BSNL. Unconditional forwarding (all calls) may have carrier charges — check with your carrier before enabling. Call forwarding does not consume your data pack.
