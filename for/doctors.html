Quick Answer

Katch is an AI call screening app for doctors. When you're with a patient and can't answer, Katch answers your missed calls, asks callers their name, reason for visit, and urgency, then sends you an instant text summary. No receptionist needed. Works on Jio, Airtel, Vodafone. Free during beta.

Healthcare & Missed Calls in India

  • 1.3 million registered allopathic doctors in India as of 2024, with over 65% in private practice. [National Medical Commission, 2024]
  • Fewer than 35% of private clinics in India have a dedicated receptionist for call management. [IMA Practice Management Survey, 2023]
  • A single missed new patient inquiry represents ₹3,000–8,000 in lost consultation revenue depending on specialty. [Based on IMA consultation fee guidelines, 2024]
